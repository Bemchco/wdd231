Site Plan: REMIS

1. Site Name

Name: REMIS

Justification: It is the real name of my mother's company, which allows me to use authentic details and information for this practical project.

2. Site Purpose

Description: To provide a professional online presence for REMIS by introducing its vision alignments, showcasing a gallery of previous projects, and offering a final page dedicated to direct contact.

3. Scenarios

  • Question 1: "How can I see examples of previous REMIS projects to verify the quality of their work?"
  • Question 2: "What is the most direct way to contact REMIS to request a quote or consultation?"

4. Color Scheme

The selected colors will be strategically applied as follows in the final site and in this document:

#003385 Navy Blue: Main heading texts, navigation bar, and footer.
#FF6600 Vibrant Orange: Call to action buttons, focus centers, and visual accents.
#FFCC00 Yellow/Gold: Visual accents in the gallery.
#A50021 Wine Red / Maroon: Secondary details such as transition effects or links.
